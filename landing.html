What it is

ORBIT maps relationships around your gravity profile — your mission, worldview, and thinking style. It finds real people: authors, researchers, and practitioners who are active in the exact domains you care about.

Every result is grounded in web search, not a database. ORBIT confirms each person exists, finds their contact info, and gives you a specific reason why they belong in your world.

Once you find them, you track them across four stages: Identified → Reached Out → Replied → Conversation. Your orbit grows around what actually moves.

How it works
  1. 01 Fill out your gravity profile. Name your mission, worldview, and what you're trying to build. This is your signal — everything ORBIT finds flows from it.
  2. 02 Get suggested people. ORBIT searches the web using exact terms from your profile — practitioners, theorists, and connectors who are genuinely active in your domain.
  3. 03 Build and manage your orbit over time. Track relationships, move people through stages, search for anyone specific, and grow your network deliberately.
